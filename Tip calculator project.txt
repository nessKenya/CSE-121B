Project Title: tip/sales tax calculator

The tip is the amount that you give as a way of appreciating the service you have received. 

Project Description
---------------------------
The tip calculator takes the billing amount and based on the type of service and number of people entered in the system, it generates the output.

Algorithm
---------------
1. Load the page of the website/app
2. Enter the total bill
3. Choose the quality of service on the drop down based on percentage
4. Enter the number of people served
5. Click the calculate buttton
6. Prints the amount
